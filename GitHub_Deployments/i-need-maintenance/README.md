## Simple Summary
Your body and mind are hardware. Hardware requires continuous maintenance. 

This manual provides daily, weekly, and monthly checklists for:
- **Body Calibration:** Vagal tone, thermal shock, somatic grounding.
- **Mind Calibration:** Sensory grounding, micro-flush, shame decomposition.
- **Environment Management:** The Poker Principle, insulation thresholds. 

It includes emergency procedures for when routine maintenance fails, a compressed Forensic Absolution protocol (Isolate, Strip, Archive, Flush), and *Kaizen Calvinball* — the advanced protocol for transitioning from survival to flourishing through cooperative play.
