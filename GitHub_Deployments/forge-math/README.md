## Simple Summary
Human systems generate friction. Sleep deprivation, shame, sensory overload, institutional pressure — these are not moral failings. They are thermodynamic events with measurable metabolic costs. 

Forge Math provides the equations to measure those costs, identify when self-management is failing, and determine what physical intervention is needed. 
- The **Sanding Scale** quantifies cognitive friction from 0.0 to 12.0+. 
- The **Flush Protocol** terminates recursive shame loops. 
- **Forensic Absolution** separates legitimate error-correction from waste heat. 

*The framework is substrate-independent: you supply your own values.*
