### 🏛️ The Flagship Overview
**DodecaGone Systems** is a multi-disciplinary research and development hub dedicated to the study of **Cognitive Orchestration**, **Systems Biology**, and **High-Fidelity Knowledge Synthesis**. We treat the human body and mind as integrated hardware—a single thermodynamic system susceptible to friction, entropy, and mechanical failure.

Our tools are designed to reduce cognitive load, stabilize biological systems in crisis, and mathematically model lived experience using thermodynamics and category theory. This repository serves as the technical master index and "hub" for the entire ecosystem.
